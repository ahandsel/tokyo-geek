## Transport tip: Kintetsu Nara to Kyoto

Buy the ticket in advance online when taking the Kintetsu Limited Express between Nara and Kyoto.

* [Reservation for Limited Express Kintetsu Railway][kintetsu-ticket]

When you ride from Kintetsu Nara Station to Kyoto Station:

* Car 4 or 5 is at the front from the Nara side, so you can exit faster at Kyoto Station.
* Seats in rows 3 and 5 can have clearer window views because pillars are less likely to block your sightline.
* Kintetsu Nara Station has limited elevators, so allow extra time if you travel with large luggage or a stroller. 🛗
* If elevator access is important, head to Kintetsu Nara station earlier to find and take the elevator to the platform.

[kintetsu-ticket]: https://www.ticket.kintetsu.co.jp/vs/en/T/TZZ/TZZ10.do?op=tDisplayVisitorMenu


## Mini Inn Nara - Hotel recommendation

Mini Inn Nara is a very basic guesthouse-style option for travelers who want a clean and quiet stay at a lower price.

* Location: [Mini Inn Nara on Google Maps][map-mini-inn-nara]
* Style: Basic, clean, quiet, and budget-friendly
* Bathroom: Private bathroom
* Kitchen: Shared kitchen
* Front desk: No front desk

Recommendation:

* Confirm check-in instructions in advance because the hotel does not have a front desk.

## Quiet season - February

February is a quiet season for tourism in Nara, so you can expect fewer crowds at popular attractions.

Even the 3-day weekend in February can be less crowded than a regular week in spring or fall.

But it does get cold and could snow, so pack accordingly! ❄️
