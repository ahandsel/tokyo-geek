## Friday - head to and explore Shuzenji


### Morning

* [Shinagawa Station][shinagawa-station] → [Mishima Station][mishima-station] via Shinkansen
  * **Train:** Tokaido Shinkansen
  * **Time:** 50 min
  * **Fare:** 4,400 JPY per person
* [Mishima Station][mishima-station] → [Shuzenji Station][shuzenji-station] via train
  * **Train:** Izuhakone Tetsudo-Sunzu Line
  * **Time:** 40 min
  * **Fare:** 550 JPY per person
* [Shuzenji Station][shuzenji-station] → [Shuzenji Onsen Bus Stop][shuzenji-onsen-bus-stop] via C10 bus
  * **Bus:** `Ｃ１０：修善寺駅~修善寺温泉修善寺温泉行`
  * **Time:** 10 min
  * **Fare:** 260 JPY per person


### Afternoon

* [Shuzenji Niji-no-Sato][shuzenji-niji-no-sato]
* [Bamboo Forest Path][bamboo-forest-path]
* Enjoy a **morning walk** to experience the local autumn scenery.


### Evening

* [Stay at Onsen Yado Mizuguchi / 茶処・温泉宿水ぐち][onsen-yado-mizuguchi]


## Saturday - head to Hakone


### Morning

* **Head to Mishima Skywalk**
  * [Shuzenji Station][shuzenji-station] → [Mishima Station][mishima-station] via train
  * [Mishima Station][mishima-station] → [Mishima Skywalk][mishima-skywalk-map] via `Ｎ６５：三島駅~ＦＰ~元箱根港` bus
* [Mishima Sky Walk][mishima-sky-walk]
  * Additional Info: [Hakone Navi][hakone-navi]
  * Purchase the [Mishima 1-day ticket][mishima-1-day-ticket] via the [EMot][emot-app] app for discounts.


### Afternoon

* **Head to Hakone**
  * [Mishima Skywalk][mishima-skywalk-map] → [Hakone Tokaido Checkpoint][hakone-tokaido-checkpoint] via `Ｎ６５：三島駅~ＦＰ~元箱根港` bus
  * ⚠️ There are only a few [N65 三島駅→FP→スカイウォーク→元箱根港 (8650011) - バスルート][n65-bus-route] buses per day. Plan accordingly.
* [Onshi-Hakone Park / 神奈川県立恩賜箱根公園][onshi-hakone-park]
* Hiking the Old Trail
  * From [権現坂 祠][gongenzaka-shrine] to [Hata-juku Ichirizuka (Tokaido Road Distance Marker) / 畑宿一里塚（箱根旧街道）][hata-juku-ichirizuka]
  * YAMAP Rout example: [箱根旧街道 ~芦ノ湖から奥湯本 / あにーさんの箱根山・神山の活動データ][yamap-route]


### Evening

* Stay overnight at Hakone


## Sunday - explore Hakone and return to Tokyo


### Morning

* **Hakone Komagatake Ropeway**
  * Visit the top of Komagatake Peak of the Mt. Hakone
  * Start: [Hakone-en Station][hakone-en-station]
  * End: [Komagatake Ropeway Top Station][komagatake-ropeway-top-station]
  * [Komagatake Ropeway - Hakone Travel][komagatake-ropeway-guide]


### Afternoon

* **Hakone Ropeway (Main)**
  * Start: [Togendai Station][togendai-station]
  * Visit: [Hakone Ropeway Owakudani Station][owakudani-station]
    * See [Owakudani][owakudani-station]
    * Walk on the [Kamiyama Mountain Climbing Course][kamiyama-climbing-course]
  * End: [Sōunzan Station][sounzan-station]


### Evening

Return to Shinjuku via the Romance Car.

* Reserve Romance Car from Hakone to Shinjuku via [www.web-odakyu.com/e-romancecar/][e-romancecar]


## Reference
