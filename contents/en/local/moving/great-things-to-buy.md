## Mini dryer for clothes

In general, most people dry their clothes using a drying rack on their balcony. But having a small dryer can be very helpful. Especially for:

* Rainy season (June and July) when outdoor drying is difficult.
* Winter months when drying outside takes a long time.
* Towels and thicker items that take a long time to dry.
* Solid size for a small apartment: 3.0 kg to 4.0 kg

Search terms:

* Compact clothes dryer = `小型衣類乾燥機`
* Energy saving = `省電力`
* Clothes dryer = `衣類乾燥機`
* Small size = `小型`
* Compact = `コンパクト`

## Dehumidifier

A dehumidifier is very useful during the rainy season and winter months to keep your apartment dry and prevent mold growth.

* Dehumidifier = `除湿機`
