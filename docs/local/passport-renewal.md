## About passport renewal applications

Individuals who hold a valid passport may apply for passport renewal.


## Eligible applicants

The following individuals are eligible to apply:

* Individuals whose registered domicile, name, and gender in the family register have not changed.
* Individuals whose passport has less than one year of validity remaining, or whose passport has three or fewer double-page spreads remaining on the visa pages.
  * Depending on the destination country, a remaining validity period that is longer than the planned length of stay may be required. Please be careful if you renew your passport without changing the expiration date printed on your current passport.
* Individuals whose My Number Card contains their current name, address, and other personal information.
  * If the information is not up to date, please complete the update procedure at the municipal office of your place of residence.


## Required items for application

* My Number Card
* Myna Portal app installed on your smartphone
  * [iOS version][]
  * [Android version][]
* Valid passport
  * Before starting the application, please confirm your registered domicile so that you can enter it correctly.
* Photo (it will be taken with your smartphone during the application process)
* Signature on a plain white sheet of paper (it will be captured with your smartphone during the application process)


### Preparing for photo and signature capture

* When taking your facial photograph with a smartphone, please do so in a bright place with a plain, non-patterned background.
* For your signature (handwritten signature image), please sign on a plain white sheet of paper using black or blue ink.
  * Use the camera in the Myna Portal app and take the image in accordance with the on-screen guide.


## Application process

Step 1: Pre-application preparation

* Prepare your facial photograph, signature image, and application information.

Step 2: Application

* Access the [My Number Card portal][] and start the passport renewal application.
* Submit your application using the information prepared in advance.

Step 3: Confirmation of application status

* After you submit your application, you can check the subsequent procedures and the status of your application in the To-do list.
* Follow the guidance to pay the fees and receive your passport.

Step 4: Pick up your passport from the designated passport office.


## Confirm your digital certificate for signatures

A valid digital certificate for signatures and a password (6 to 16 alphanumeric characters) are required.

Please confirm that your digital certificate for signatures is valid.

* The certificate expires on the fifth birthday after it is issued.
* Please refer to the "Certificate expiration date" printed on your Individual Number Card, or the Public Individual Authentication Service portal site.
* [How to check validity][]

Please confirm the password (6 to 16 characters) for your digital certificate for signatures.

* You will need to enter the password to submit an application.
* If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it at the municipal office of your registered residence, or by downloading the dedicated application and using a kiosk terminal at a convenience store.
* [Initialize signature password at a convenience store][]


## Signature image requirements

Example of an acceptable image

* The signature is written by the person.
* The signature is fully visible and not cut off.
* The background is plain white.
* There are no stains, marks, or other blemishes on the background.
* The signature is written in dark black or blue ink.
* If the person is unable to sign, the legal representative must sign on their behalf.
