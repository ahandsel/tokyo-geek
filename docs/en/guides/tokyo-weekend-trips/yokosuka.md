## Trip overview

This long-weekend plan starts in Yokosuka (横須賀) for an island-focused day around Sarushima (猿島), then moves south to Miura (三浦市) for coastal scenery at Jogashima (城ケ島), and ends with flexible options before heading back to Tokyo.

Suggested flow:

* Day 1: Yokosuka city area + Sarushima island visit
* Day 2: Miura coast + Jogashima Park
* Day 3: Flexible nearby stops and return to Tokyo


## Day 1 - Yokosuka and Sarushima (Island day)


### Morning

* Arrive in Yokosuka and head to [Mikasa Pier / 三笠桟橋（猿島渡船乗船場）](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qSQJ1Vbbsvx4KgEu7).
* Use the pier area as your base for ferry departure and return logistics.


### Afternoon

* Visit [Sarushima / 猿島](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Wh3y5Z43rDTi18UP9).
* Walk the island trails and coastal viewpoints at a relaxed pace.
* Read the official tourism overview before your trip: [Japan Travel Sarushima reference](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1565/).


### Evening

* Return from Sarushima to Yokosuka and spend the evening in the central area.
* Keep dinner timing flexible in case island return lines are busy.


## Day 2 - Miura coast and Jogashima


### Morning

* Head from Yokosuka toward Miura (三浦市).
* Start with a coastal walk at [Jogashima Park / 城ケ島公園](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ViFoxSopEXQYMNKBA).


### Afternoon

* Continue exploring coastal viewpoints and walking paths around the Jogashima area.
* Keep this block unhurried so you can adjust for weather and crowds.


### Evening

* Return toward your accommodation area or begin the trip back toward Tokyo-side transit.
* For local marine transport and planning details, check the official reference page: [Nijiiro / うみぎょう](https://www.umigyo.co.jp/nijiiro.html).


## Day 3 - Flexible wrap-up day

Use the last day as a lighter buffer day before returning to Tokyo.

Optional nearby additions:

* Verny Park / ヴェルニー公園 - Waterfront promenade with open harbor views and an easy walking route.
* Dobuita Street / ドブ板通り - Casual street area for local atmosphere, cafes, and short browsing stops.
* Misaki Port area / 三崎港 - Port-town area known for seafood-focused meals and a slower coastal pace.


## Practical notes

* Coastal and island plans are weather dependent, so keep your day order flexible.
* Weekend lines can be longer around ferry boarding points and popular park areas.
* Wear comfortable shoes for uneven paths, stairs, and longer seaside walks.
* Check the latest operating schedules and service notices before departure.
